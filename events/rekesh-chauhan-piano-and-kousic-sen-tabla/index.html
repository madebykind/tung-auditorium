Rekesh Chauhan (Piano) and Kousic Sen (Tabla)

Multi-award-winning British pianist and composer, and iTunes World Charts Top 3 artist Rekesh Chauhan, returns to Liverpool for a concert of Indian classical music.

Lunchtime Concert
  • Wednesday 9 February 2022, 1:00pm
  • Tickets: Free (Booking Required)
Book tickets

Other info

Rekesh was awarded ‘Young Musician of the Year’ at the National Indian Arts Awards in 2018 and has collaborated with a range of musicians from Nobel Peace Prize performers to Mercury Prize Award winners. Rekesh is joined in this concert by internationally renowned tabla maestro and Milap Artist-in-Residence Kousic Sen.
